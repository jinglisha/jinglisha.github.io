    # 2、微信小程序里如何在自定义组件里创建画布

    解决：https://developers.weixin.qq.com/miniprogram/dev/component/canvas.html (opens new window)

    <canvas id="canvas" style="width:300px;height:300px;" type="2d"></canvas>

    this.createSelectorQuery()
.select('#canvas')

    node: true,
    size: true,
})

    # 3、微信小程序如何重设画布的宽高

    解决：虽然定义了style,但是画布的搞还是150，得重设一下

    canvas.width = _width;
canvas.height = _height;
